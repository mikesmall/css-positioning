Bill and Ted's Excellent Adventure

A couple of weirdos somehow get caught up with a time-travelling phone-booth, and get into all manner of shenanigans that just wouldn't fly in "Back to the Future." Thankfully, the space-time continuum appears undamaged as they do some "Deadliest Warrior" stuff, pitting history's greatest heroes against... I don't really remember how it goes.

Bill and Ted's Bogus Journey

After saving humanity from... somethingorother... Jerks from the future send Evil Robot Bill And Ted to murder the real Bill And Ted. You may be familiar with this idea from "Terminator: Genisys" and other Terminator films. Anyway: Bill and Ted get murdered by their robot doppelgangers, and then defeat the Grim Reaper at a few old-timey board games, in Hell. Somehow, this ends with our heroes wearing long beards, and singing one of the worst KISS songs in a high school gym. Unless that's how the first movie ended...
